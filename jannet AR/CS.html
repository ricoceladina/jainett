About CS

CS OBJECTIVES

1. To equip students with the skills and competences in information communications technology, its operation language, and design.

2. To empower would-be IT and computer science professionals with the latest trends and developments in ICT, enabling them to meet the needs of society.

3. To provide adequate instruction, training, and workshops for office workers and practitioners on the use of IT in organizational operations.

4. To prepare graduates with a solid foundation in programming languages, systems, and tools needed in the global corporate environment.

CS MISSION

Build a strong foundation for providing quality education in both theoretical and applied aspects of computer science. We aim to train students to apply this education to solve real-world problems, equipping them with the skills to excel in global 21st-century work environments.

CS HISTORY

The history of computer science began long before the modern discipline of computer science emerged in the 20th century. Early tools for computation, like the abacus, were developed in ancient times. The abacus, designed between 2700 and 2300 BCE in Sumer, was used to perform basic calculations and is still in use in some parts of the world today.

The progression from mechanical inventions and mathematical theories towards modern machines formed a major academic field, leading to the creation of massive industries worldwide.

The College of Computer Science at The University of Manila is committed to advancing computer science education and research. Our programs are designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in both theoretical and applied fields.
